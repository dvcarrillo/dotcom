My story

My story

My journey into Product Design, driven by a lifelong passion for technology, began with a Bacherlor's degree in Computer Engineering from the University of Granada. This led me to over two years as a software engineer in Bordeaux, collaborating with excellent design teams at Thales and AGFA HealthCare.

In 2020, I moved to Stockholm to pursue a Master's degree in Interactive Media Technology at KTH Royal Institute of Technology, transitioning into UX design within the vibrant startup scene. Since graduating in 2022, I have continued to grow as a designer in Bordeaux and Paris, currently leading design projects at Societe Generale CIB, focusing on responsiveness and data-heavy services.

My unique blend of technical expertise, meticulous attention to detail, and creative ambition enables me to take design projects from concept to completion. With a deep, holistic understanding of the design process, I am committed to crafting solutions that meet business goals while truly putting users first.
